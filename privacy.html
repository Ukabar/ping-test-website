Privacy Policy

Professional privacy information for Ping Test: Gaming Speed.

Effective year: 2026

Ping Test: Gaming Speed is designed to help users estimate network quality through HTTP-based network tests. This Privacy Policy explains what the app does, what it stores locally, and how advertising services may process data.

Network Tests

The app performs HTTP-based measurements to estimate connection quality. These tests may include HTTP latency, jitter, download speed, packet loss estimation, and a gaming score based on measured conditions.

Local Preferences

The app stores local preferences using SharedPreferences. These preferences may include app settings and user choices saved on your device. They are used to improve the app experience and do not require account creation.

Advertising

Google AdMob is used to display advertisements in the app. Google may collect and process data according to its own privacy policy, advertising policies, and consent requirements.

Accounts and Permissions

Ping Test: Gaming Speed does not require account creation. The app does not request unnecessary permissions and only uses capabilities needed to provide its network testing and app experience.
